CORK SLANG



#1. Mint

Cool or great



#2. Bazzer

Haircut

Christ will you look at the bazzer on your man!



#3. Gat

An alcoholic beverage

Going gatting with the boys Tuesday night, gonna be mint!



#4. Doing a line

Maintaining a steady and commited relationship

No surprise they're engaged, sure they've been doing a line since they shifted in third class!



#5. Langer

A complete idiot

He's an absolute langer



#6. Langers

To be intoxicated

He ws so langers he couldn't even open his eyes to find his pint



#7. Haunted

Lucky or fortunate

Lad 1: Hust after winning a fiver on a scratchcard!

Lad 2: Ah you're haunted, that's the second time this month!



#8. Flah

An attractive person

Jimmy: God that Emma Watson is an absolute flah

John: Is it weird that I've been to see Beauty and the Beast three times?



#9. Out with me

To be annoyed at or ignoring someone

Boy 1: Why didn't Sophie say hello back there?

Boy 2: Ah she's been out with me since I got langers at the weekend and drunk texted her



#10. Jag

To go on a date

Sister: Where's Sean gone?

Mother: He's gone to meet a girl! Isn't it great?

Sister: Jaysus never thought my little brother would be off on a jag. Hashtag forever alone.
