10 BELFAST SLANG WORDS AND PHRASES EXPLAINED IN ENGLISH

#1. Talent

Good looking females/males

She has a lot of talent

#2. Canny

Cannot

I canny go out tonight

#3. Critter

Used to describe someone that you have pity for

Look at him out in the rain, the poor critter

#4. Belly up

Unsuccessful

Boy: How's the new business going?

Girl: Terrible, it's gone belly up already

#5. Swallied/Half-cut / Cut / Full

To be drunk

I was seriously swallied last night

Boy 1: I'm starting to feel a bit cut lads

Boy 2: Feeling half-cut myself

Boy 3: Me too lads, starting to get full

#6. Ride

Attractive female or male

He's such a ride

#7. Swally

A drink

Do you wanna go for a swally after work?

#8. See

Engage in a relationship with the opposite sex

Girl 1: You're gonig out with him?

Girl 2: Yea, we've been seeing each other for a week now

#9. Away on!

You must be joking!

Boy: You'll never guess who I just met...David Beckham!

Man: Away on with ye!

#10. What about ye?

As in how are you doing my good man

Yeah, yeah I'm doing well, what about ye?
