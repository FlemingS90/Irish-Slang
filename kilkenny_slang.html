10 KILKENNY SLANG WORDS AND PHRASES EXPLAINED IN ENGLISH

#1. Face like the back of a bus

An unattractive person

"Sure he has a face like the back of a bus"

#2. I Wounldnt Ride 'er Inta Battle

I would not have sex with her

"The head on 'er i wouldnt ride her inta battle"

#3. Eat The Head Off

To verbally attack someone

Example: ”Oh lads Mammy is gonna eat the head off me when she finds out I’ve lost my phone again”.

#4. Dry Shite

A very very very very dull person.

Example: ”Will ya give up givin’ out?! You’re nothin’ but a dry shite”.

#5.Now your doin it, pickin ur nose n chewin it

Your now doing it the way it should be done. You are going in the right direction

"Ye were losing the game but now yer doin it, pickin yer nose n chewin it"

#6.Sound as a pound

To be alright

"I am sound as a pound"

#7.‘Hows a goan?'

How are you?

"Well Jimmy! Hows a goan?!"

#8. Acting The Maggot

To fool around.

Example: ‘‘Ah give up acting the maggot now and get back to work”.

#9. Shower

A group of people you dislike

Example: ”Fine Gael are only a shower of criminals that’s all”.

#10. There’s A Want In Him

Someone who is a bit weird

Example: ”Is that an earring in his ear? Jaysus there must be a bit of a want in him”.
