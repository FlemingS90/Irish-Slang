LIMERICK SLANG

#1. Tackies

Tracksuits

No way am I jumping that wall, these tackies cost me a fortune!



#2. Beour

An attractive woman, thought to originate from "beautiful"

C'mere I must go, I'm meeting me beour in town



#3. Daycent

It's great!



#4. Fair Daycent

Quite excellent!



#5. Pure Daycent

Incredible!



#6. UNREAL

Pronounced "unrale", this means the most sublime thing which can exist.

That new Honda Civic that Pa's after getting is unreal



#7. Aboy the kid

Well done, congratulations or just a friendly compliment



#8. Ghoulbag

An unpleasant person

Ah kid, can't face work on Monday, shower of ghoulbags in that office



#9. Peel an orange in his pocket

Someone stingy, or mean (pronounced mane)

Kid 1: That ghoulbag is after dodging his round again

Kid 2: Sure what d'ya expect, he'd peel an orange in his pocket that lad.



#10. Put the heart crossways in you

To give a thoroughly unpleasnt shock

Pa drives past loudly his new Subaru

Biddy 1: Christ almighty theres some hum of that car

Biddy 2: Sure jaysus it has the heart put crossways in me!
