10 DONEGAL SLANG WORDS AND PHRASES EXPLAINED IN ENGLISH

#1. Open a can o' worms

To make a situation worse than what it is

Girl 1: I can't believe you told her what happened last night

Girl 2: Yeah, you really opened a can of worms there

#2. Stout /Deadly / Class

Used to describe anything good!! can be used for something bad but generally good! It is also used to agree with a question or just answer with stout!!

This DJ is stout

This bar is deadly

The music was class

#3. Shuteye

To get some sleep

Man 1: Are you coming out tonight?

Man 2: Naw I'm knackered, I'm going to get some shuteye

#4. Well oiled / Clane / Steamed up

To be drunk

You were well oiled / clane / steamed up last night

#5. Acting da maggot

Messing around

Woman: What happened to the window?

Man: Aw the lads were acting the maggot

#6. Aulfilla

Father

How's your aulfilla doing these days?

#7. Nay

No

Girl 1: Are you going to school tomorrow?

Girl 2: Nay

#8. Out in da sticks

Used to describe a location miles from the nearest town

I live way out in the sticks

#9. Raining cats and dogs

Raining heavily

Take a jacket, It's raining cats and dogs out there

#10. On da tear

Drinking a lot of alcohol in one night or over a few days

I went on the tear last night, was great craic but I'm feeling it today
